Private Luxury - St. Lucia

Perched cliffside overlooking the majestic pitons and Soufriere bay in Saint Lucia, Caille Blanc Villa & Hotel offers guests an uncommon luxury experience with personalized stays in each of its six uniquely appointed suites.

Each suite is decorated with treasures from around the world which accent the locally crafted hand hewn four poster beds with safari netting. Open the doors to the suites to view Saint Lucia's glorious pitons and bask in the privacy of individual pools. Take a dip in the 65 foot infinity pool where the waters converge with the sky and rainbows reflect from above.

Guests enjoy the complimentary breakfast daily as well as local specialties. The Pavilion restaurant provides lunch and transforms at night to provide romantic candlelight dinners. The on-site services include a cultural immersion in Saint Lucia's gastronomic and farming resources with lessons in coal pot cooking and chocolate making.
